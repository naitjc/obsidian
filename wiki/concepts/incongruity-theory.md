# Incongruity Theory

Incongruity theory explains humor and sarcasm as tension between expected and observed meanings.

## Relevance to NLP
- Supports multimodal sarcasm detection where image and text conflict.
- Helps design features for pragmatic meaning beyond literal interpretation.
