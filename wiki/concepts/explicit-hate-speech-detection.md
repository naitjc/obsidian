# Explicit Hate Speech Detection

Explicit hate speech detection targets direct hateful expressions with overt slurs or explicit derogatory language.

## Role in Pipeline
- Often easier than implicit hate detection because lexical cues are stronger.
- Useful as a baseline layer in moderation pipelines before implicit/contextual modules.
