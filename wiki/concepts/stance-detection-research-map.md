# Stance Detection Research Map

## Scope

This direction covers stance classification toward targets, zero-shot and open-domain stance detection, conversational stance, multimodal stance, target-aware adaptation, bias, and LLM reasoning or verification for stance decisions.

## Synthesis Notes
- The central pressure in this direction is generalization to unseen targets, topics, domains, languages, and conversational contexts.
- LLM-based work splits into two families: generating or augmenting training data, and reasoning/verification at inference time.
- Target formulation is not a minor detail; target absence, gendered targets, stereotypes, and entity-to-entity settings can materially change conclusions.
- Multimodal stance work is smaller than text-only zero-shot work but important for image-text and structurally enriched stance settings.
