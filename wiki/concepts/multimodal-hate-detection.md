# Multimodal Hate Detection

Detecting hate speech in multimodal content, especially memes combining image and text.

## Key Approaches

1. **Graph Neural Networks** - TOT (Zhang et al. 2023) uses topology-aware optimal transport
2. **Optimal Transport** - Modeling cross-modal relationships
3. **Retrieval-Augmented Methods** - Mei et al. 2024, 2025 use retrieval-guided contrastive learning
4. **Chain-of-Evolution Prompting** - Huang et al. 2025 evolve prompts for LMMs

## Datasets

- **Hateful Memes** (Facebook) - Standard benchmark for multimodal hate detection
- **ToxiCN** (Bai et al. 2025) - Chinese span-level toxicity extraction benchmark
